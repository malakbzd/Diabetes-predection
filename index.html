Understanding Diabetes

Type 2 Diabetes

Type 2 diabetes is a chronic condition where the body doesn't use insulin properly. It accounts for 90-95% of all diabetes cases.

Risk Factors:
  • Overweight or obesity
  • Age 45 or older
  • Family history of diabetes
  • Physical inactivity
Prediabetes

Prediabetes means blood sugar is higher than normal but not high enough for diabetes diagnosis.

Key Numbers:
  • HbA1c: 5.7% to 6.4%
  • Fasting glucose: 100-125 mg/dL
  • 1 in 3 adults has prediabetes
  • Up to 70% progress to diabetes
Complications

Uncontrolled diabetes can lead to serious complications:

  • Heart disease: 2x higher risk
  • Kidney damage: Leading cause of kidney failure
  • Vision loss: Diabetic retinopathy
  • Nerve damage: Neuropathy
